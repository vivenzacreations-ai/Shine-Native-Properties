Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send my Varmala for preservation?

Once you place the order, we will share detailed instructions and our studio address. You need to courier the dried/fresh flowers to us in a well-ventilated cardboard box.

What is the processing time?

Resin art requires precision and multiple pouring layers to cure properly. Please allow 15-20 business days for custom varmala pieces and 7-10 days for standard decor.

Do you accept returns or cancellations?

Because every piece is 100% custom-made and personalized, we strictly do not accept returns, refunds, or cancellations once the order is confirmed and production begins.
